Tasting Notes

Rosa premium tequila is the most sought-after party guest. Her reputation precedes her, known for her delicately sweet manner and bright presence. Rosa tequila has yet to meet someone who she couldn't win over.

Packaged in a proprietary, rose gold lip-shaped bottle with Queen's crown stopper.
