The Story Behind the Bottle

Every bottle of BIATCH is more than tequila — it's a jewel forged for women who refuse to be small. Premium, unapologetic, and crafted to celebrate bold elegance, our collection crowns every occasion with power and grace. This isn't just what you drink — it's what you stand for.

How She's Made

From Agave Field to Your Glass

Deep in the highlands of Jalisco, every BIATCH bottle begins its journey among the volcanic soils of Los Altos. Our Blue Weber Agave plants mature 7–10 years before harvest — coaxed to peak sweetness by altitude, mineral-rich earth, and generations of craft.

The Founder

Meet Sue. The woman who changed the pour.

Sue Hrib, a seasoned entrepreneur with a proven track record of success, launched BIATCH tequila with a mission to empower women. After building and selling a successful consulting firm, Sue channeled her passion for community service — honed through a decade of work with the Carter Center and Usher's New Look — into reclaiming the strong female spirit "BIATCH". BIATCH is not just about quality spirits; it's about making a meaningful impact.

"I wanted to create a tequila for the woman who owns every room she walks into."

— Sue, Founder of BIATCH Tequila
