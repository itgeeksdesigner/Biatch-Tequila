About BIATCH

The Story Behind the Bottle

Our Story

It started with a dare — and became a movement.

BIATCH Tequila was born from a moment of defiance. Tired of a spirits industry that overlooked women — both as founders and as drinkers — our team decided to create something unapologetically theirs. The name? Reclaimed. The spirit? Elevated. The mission? Unmistakable.

The Founder

Meet Sue. The woman who changed the pour.

Sue is a force. A serial entrepreneur, tequila lover, and relentless advocate for women in business, she launched BIATCH after years in the spirits industry watching women get passed over. She didn't wait for permission. She built the brand she always wanted to drink.

"I wanted to create a tequila for the woman who owns every room she walks into."

— Sue, Founder of BIATCH Tequila
Our Mission

Empowering women, one bottle at a time.

A percentage of every bottle sold goes directly to organizations supporting women entrepreneurs worldwide. Because the revolution isn't just in the glass — it's in what we do with what we pour.

The Craft

From blue agave to your glass.

Our tequila is made from 100% Blue Weber Agave, harvested by hand in the highlands of Jalisco. Cooked slowly in traditional brick ovens, double-distilled in copper pot stills, and rested to perfection. No additives. No shortcuts. Just honest, extraordinary tequila.
